About Bukidnon

Bukidnon, often referred to as the 'Highland Paradise,' is a landlocked province in the heart of the island of Mindanao in the Philippines. Known for its stunning landscapes, Bukidnon is a haven for nature lovers and adventure seekers. With its picturesque rolling hills, verdant pine forests, and rich biodiversity, the province offers visitors a refreshing escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. Bukidnon is home to the indigenous Bukidnon people, who have a rich history and vibrant culture deeply rooted in the land. The province boasts a range of unique tourist attractions, including the famous Del Monte pineapple plantation, the breathtaking Dahilayan Adventure Park, and the mesmerizing Monastery of Transfiguration. No matter what type of traveler you are, Bukidnon has something for everyone to explore and enjoy.
